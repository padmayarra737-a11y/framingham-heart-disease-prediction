# Framingham Heart Disease Prediction

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on predicting the **10-year risk of coronary heart disease (CHD)** using the Framingham dataset.  
It applies Machine Learning techniques to analyze patient health data and identify individuals at risk of developing heart disease.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Build a predictive model for heart disease risk  
- Analyze key health factors influencing CHD  
- Evaluate model performance using standard metrics  

---

## 📊 Dataset Description
The **Framingham Heart Disease Dataset** is a collection of patient data from a long-term cardiovascular study conducted in Framingham, Massachusetts.

- 📁 Total Records: 4000+  
- 📌 Attributes: 15  
- 🎯 Target: 10-year risk of Coronary Heart Disease (CHD)  

---

## 🔑 Key Features

### 👤 Patient Demographics
- Gender  
- Age  
- Education level  

### 🩺 Health Metrics
- Smoking habits  
- Blood pressure  
- Cholesterol levels  
- Body Mass Index (BMI)  
- Glucose levels  

### 📜 Medical History
- History of stroke  
- Hypertension  
- Diabetes  

### 🎯 Target Variable
- Risk of developing coronary heart disease in 10 years
